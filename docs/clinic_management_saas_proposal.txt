CLINIC MANAGEMENT SAAS PROJECT PROPOSAL

Prepared For:
Clinic Management Platform Stakeholders

Prepared On:
March 31, 2026

1. Executive Summary

This proposal presents a Software as a Service (SaaS) based Clinic Management application designed to digitize and streamline healthcare operations across multiple roles in a single connected platform. The system currently supports admins, doctors, assistants, patients, and pharmacies through one integrated workflow. It combines appointment handling, patient coordination, medicine management, cart and order processing, role-based dashboards, revenue tracking, and operational reporting.

The platform is structured as a multi-role healthcare operations system where each stakeholder accesses the features relevant to their responsibilities. This makes the application suitable for clinics, healthcare networks, pharmacy partners, and growing healthcare businesses looking for a scalable SaaS solution.

2. Project Vision

The vision of this project is to create a centralized digital healthcare ecosystem that improves efficiency, visibility, and service quality. Instead of handling appointments, pharmacy sales, stock management, and coordination through disconnected manual processes, this system enables all participants to operate within one platform.

The application is designed to:
- improve patient experience
- simplify internal clinic workflows
- support pharmacy order operations
- provide role-based accountability
- generate revenue insights for business decisions
- support future SaaS growth across multiple organizations

3. SaaS Positioning

This application functions as a SaaS platform because it is built around centralized access, role-based user management, operational dashboards, and reusable workflows that can serve multiple clinics and healthcare stakeholders from one product foundation.

The current system already reflects SaaS characteristics:
- multi-user, multi-role access control
- central admin oversight
- modular healthcare service workflows
- digital ordering and appointment lifecycle management
- business analytics and downloadable reports
- scalable architecture for future tenant expansion

4. Core Modules Implemented

The platform currently includes the following modules:

Admin Module
- centralized access to platform records
- overview of clinics, doctors, pharmacies, assistants, medicines, orders, and appointments
- revenue monitoring from pharmacy and doctor operations
- PDF reporting and filtered analytics

Doctor Module
- doctor profile and fee management
- appointment visibility
- doctor dashboard with completed appointment revenue
- patient and appointment activity insights

Assistant Module
- appointment request handling
- appointment coordination support
- assistant dashboard with activity and revenue share visibility

Patient Module
- registration and login
- medicine browsing
- cart management
- checkout workflow
- personal order tracking
- appointment request capability

Pharmacy Module
- medicine creation and stock handling
- selling price and purchase price tracking
- pharmacy-specific order visibility
- order status updates
- sales and inventory dashboard
- PDF export for pharmacy orders

Order and Commerce Module
- add to cart with stock validation
- quantity control with stock checks
- checkout UI and order creation flow
- order cancellation rules
- delivered-order based revenue calculations
- automatic stock reduction after order placement

5. Key Business Workflows

The application supports the following important operational flows:

Patient and Appointment Flow
- patient sends appointment request
- assistant reviews and processes the request
- appointment is created and tracked
- doctor manages appointment outcomes
- completed appointments contribute to role-based revenue reporting

Medicine and Pharmacy Flow
- pharmacy adds medicines with purchase price, selling price, and stock
- patient adds medicines to cart
- checkout creates order
- pharmacy sees only relevant orders
- pharmacy updates delivery lifecycle
- delivered orders drive revenue and profit analytics

Administrative Oversight Flow
- admin monitors total system activity
- admin reviews combined income sources
- admin filters analytics by period
- admin downloads structured PDF reports

6. Revenue Model

The current business logic supports platform monetization and financial tracking.

Pharmacy Revenue Model
- pharmacy revenue is based on delivered orders
- gross profit is calculated from selling price minus purchase price
- super admin share is calculated from pharmacy profit
- pharmacy dashboard shows revenue, profit, stock, and order performance

Doctor Revenue Model
- doctor revenue is based on completed appointments
- admin share is calculated from doctor fee revenue
- assistant share is also calculated from completed appointment revenue
- doctor dashboard displays earnings, profit allocation, and operational data

Admin Revenue Visibility
- admin dashboard combines pharmacy-side and doctor-side revenue monitoring
- separate filtered reports are available for pharmacy and doctor performance
